About Founder and CEO

Mr. Shivjeet Jaiswal

Founder, SMJ Speed Logistic Solutions Pvt Ltd | Director, Highway on Wheels Transport Pvt Ltd (HOWTPL)

Professional Background:

With over 11 years of experience in the logistics industry, Shivjeet Jaiswal has established himself as a visionary leader and an expert in supply chain management. His journey began with a profound interest in optimizing logistics processes, leading him to significant roles in prominent logistics companies.

As the Director of HOWTPL , he has been pivotal in transforming the company into a leading player in the industry, renowned for its innovative approaches and commitment to excellence.

Major Milestones in his journey:

2013: Started operations in food grains transportation.

2018: Expanded into logistics and infrastructure, beginning with two major clients(JSW Cement,ACC Cement) in HOWTPL.

2019-2024:Achieved significant growth in turnover and operations in HOWTPL including:

   -As of March 2024, we have lifted more than 20lacs MT, and our turnover has reached 202 crores.

   -Growth driven by deploying owned vehicles, conducting in-house operations, and adopting digitalization products like GPS,Fastag & Automation     in operations.
